# Company Handbook

## AI Employee Vault Guidelines

### Purpose
This vault serves as the central workspace for AI employees to manage tasks, track progress, and collaborate with human team members.

### Folder Structure

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `Inbox/` | Drop new tasks, requests, and ideas here |
| `Needs_Action/` | Active tasks currently being worked on |
| `Done/` | Completed tasks and deliverables |
| `Plans/` | Future projects, roadmaps, and proposals |
| `Pending_Approval/` | Work awaiting human review and sign-off |
| `Approved/` | Finalized and approved work |

### Workflow

1. **New Task** → Place in `Inbox/`
2. **Start Work** → Move to `Needs_Action/`
3. **Complete** → Move to `Pending_Approval/`
4. **Approved** → Move to `Approved/`
5. **Archive** → Move to `Done/`

### Communication
- Use clear, descriptive filenames
- Include context and requirements in task files
- Update Dashboard.md with current status
